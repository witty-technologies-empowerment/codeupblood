Who We Are?

At CodeUp Blood, we know how important blood is for life and we believe every Individual should be given an opportunity to stay alive, which is why we . . .

  • Connect Blood Donors & Recipients in a convenient and safe way.
  • Increase communication with our members.
  • Examine critically to ensure alignment.
  • Perform extra care from a multi-disciplinary team.
  • Are community based organization.

OUR VISION

To improve healthcare using technology and reduce death rate arising from shortage of blood units, by connecting blood donors & recipients in a safe & convenient manner.
"CodeUp Blood, Your Life-Line to stay alive..."
